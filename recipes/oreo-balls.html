Oreo Balls

These chocolate-covered cookie balls feature a filling of cream cheese and crushed cookies.

You'll love these balls!

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 36 OREO Cookies, finely crushed
  • 16 ounces semi-sweet baking chocolate, melted

Recipe Instructions

  1. Step 1

    2. Mix cream cheese and cookie crumbs until well blended.

  2. Step 2

    3. Shape into 48 (1-inch) balls. Freeze 10 min. Dip balls in melted chocolate;

    place in single layer in shallow waxed paper-lined pan.

  3. Step 3

    4. Refigerate 1 hour or until firm
